Who We Are

Huntsville Holding Company is a locally-owned property management firm founded by graduates of the University of Alabama in Huntsville (UAH). As former college students ourselves, we understand the unique challenges and frustrations that come with renting as a student—from tight budgets to maintenance issues and communication gaps with landlords.

That's why we've built our business around working directly with both students and parents. We know that finding the right rental home during your college years can be stressful, and we're here to make that process easier. Whether you're a student looking for your first house or a parent helping your child find safe, affordable housing near campus, we work with you every step of the way.

Our team brings firsthand experience of what it's like to be a college renter in Huntsville, and we're committed to providing transparent communication, responsive maintenance, and fair, flexible leasing terms that work for students and their families.
